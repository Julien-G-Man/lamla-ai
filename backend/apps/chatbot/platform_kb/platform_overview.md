# Lamla Platform Overview

## What Lamla Is
Keywords: lamla, platform, overview, purpose, ai tutor, learning app
Lamla is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help students study faster and with better retention.

The core experience combines:
- AI tutor chat for explanations and guidance
- Quiz generation from study material
- Flashcard generation and study review
- Progress tracking through dashboard analytics

## Who It Is For
Keywords: students, learners, education, university, school
Lamla is designed primarily for students and independent learners who want structured study support and quick revision tools.

## Main Value
Keywords: value, benefits, revision, retention, productivity
Lamla helps users:
- turn notes into practice quickly
- understand difficult topics in simpler language
- track performance and consistency
- revise with less manual work
