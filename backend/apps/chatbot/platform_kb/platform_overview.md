# Lamla Platform Overview

## What Lamla Is
Keywords: lamla, platform, overview, purpose, ai tutor, learning app, edtech, study platform, exam prep
Lamla AI is an AI-powered learning platform that helps students study smarter and ace their exams. Upload your study materials and transform them into interactive quizzes and flashcards, or chat with an AI tutor available 24/7.

**Core features:**
- **AI Tutor** - Get instant explanations and personalized study guidance anytime
- **Quiz Generator** - Create practice quizzes from your study materials with automatic scoring
- **Flashcards** - Generate flashcard decks with built-in spaced repetition tracking
- **Materials Library** - Share and download community study resources
- **Progress Dashboard** - Track your learning activity and performance over time

## Main Navigation
Keywords: navigation, menu, navbar, where to find, how to access, main menu
The main navigation menu is at the top of every page:

- **Home** - Landing page with platform information
- **AI Tutor** - Chat interface to ask the AI study assistant questions
- **Quiz** - Create and take practice quizzes
- **Flashcards** - Manage and study your flashcard decks
- **Dashboard** - View your study stats and activity
- **Login/Logout** - Access your account

Click any menu item to navigate to that feature.

## Who It's For
Keywords: students, learners, education, university, school, independent learning, exam preparation
Lamla AI is designed for:
- University and college students preparing for exams
- High school students reviewing course material
- Independent learners mastering new subjects
- Anyone needing AI-powered study assistance

## Key Benefits
Keywords: value, benefits, revision, retention, productivity, time saving, study smarter
Lamla AI helps you:
- **Save time** - Auto-generate quizzes and flashcards from your notes instead of manual creation
- **Study smarter** - Get AI explanations for concepts you don't understand
- **Track progress** - See your improvement and identify weak areas
- **Learn actively** - Practice with questions rather than passive reading
- **Study anywhere** - Access your materials and AI tutor from any device

## Getting Started
Keywords: getting started, how to start, first steps, begin, sign up and start
1. Click **Signup** in the top navigation
2. Create your account with email and password
3. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
4. Log in and start using features
5. Navigate using the top menu to access different tools
