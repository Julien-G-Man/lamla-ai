# Learning Tools

## Quiz Generator
Keywords: quiz, quiz generator, test, exam practice, questions
Lamla can generate quizzes from study material to help users practice and evaluate understanding.

Typical quiz capabilities include:
- auto-generated questions from content
- scoring and result feedback
- history for performance review

## Flashcards
Keywords: flashcards, deck, spaced repetition, revision cards, memorization
Lamla supports flashcard creation for active recall and revision.

Users can:
- generate flashcards from content
- save decks
- review cards over time

## AI Tutor Chat
Keywords: chatbot, ai tutor, assistant, ask question, explanation
The AI Tutor answers learning questions, explains concepts, and supports day-to-day study sessions.

## Dashboard And Progress
Keywords: dashboard, progress, analytics, stats, activity
Dashboard views provide learning activity signals such as quizzes completed, study usage, and engagement trends.
