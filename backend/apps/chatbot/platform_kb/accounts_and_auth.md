# Accounts And Authentication

## Sign Up And Login
Keywords: signup, sign up, login, account, username, email, password
Users can create an account and log in using either:
- email and password
- username and password

## Email Verification
Keywords: email verification, verify email, verification link, resend verification
After sign up, users may be asked to verify their email to unlock full account trust and security workflows.

## Profile Management
Keywords: profile, update profile, profile image, avatar, account settings
Users can:
- update profile details
- upload a profile image
- manage personal account settings

## Password Security
Keywords: password reset, change password, password security, credentials
Users can change their password from authenticated account settings.

Auth endpoints are protected by rate limiting to reduce brute-force and abuse attempts.
