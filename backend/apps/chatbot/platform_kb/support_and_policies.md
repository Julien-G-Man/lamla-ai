## Getting Faster Help
Keywords: response time, support delay, ticket, follow up, help tips, faster response
To help us resolve your issue quickly:

**When contacting support, include:**
- A clear description of the problem
- What you were trying to do
- Any error messages you saw (exact text or screenshot)
- What device/browser you're using
- Your account email (if applicable)

**Response times:**
Support response times vary depending on request volume. We aim to respond as quickly as possible.

**Before contacting support:**
- Try refreshing the page
- Check if your file meets size limits (10MB max)
- Verify your internet connection
- Try a different browser if possible
